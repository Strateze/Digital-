Our Mission

Strateze exists to translate digital behaviour into real-world opportunity.

The internet has created an entirely new economy.

Millions of young people grow up immersed in digital platforms, culture and communities. Yet traditional education systems rarely teach how these experiences translate into real skills or career opportunities.

Strateze exists to close that gap.

Founder Story

Built From Lived Experience

Strateze was founded by Ezeani Inyama after helping build a global sports media platform reaching millions of young people.

Through collaborations with creators, athletes and sports organisations, he gained first-hand insight into how the digital economy shapes Generation Z. As a Gen Z founder, he understands both youth culture and the evolving digital landscape.

Ezeani's Track Record

  • ✓ Co-built a global sports media platform surpassing 4 million followers
  • ✓ Contributed to one of the most viewed TikTok series in sports history
  • ✓ Generated over 500 million views
  • ✓ Worked with elite athletes, global brands and major sports organisations

The Vision

Strateze aims to build global digital literacy programmes that empower young people everywhere.

By combining education, sport and culture, the platform seeks to create accessible pathways into the modern digital economy.
